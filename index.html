KMSKA

In cooperation with the Royal Museum of Fine Arts in Antwerp. We made a web app in a group of five students.
The app was build to help you plan a route during your visit to the museum. You would first take a small quiz and based on your interests the app would calculate a personalised route for you.
Or you could select one of our predetermined routes. The app was coded in HTML, CSS and Javascript; we used multiple libraries such as Imagga, Recombee and Pathfinding.js for the AI to calculate the best routes.
